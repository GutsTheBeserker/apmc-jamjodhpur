🌾 APMC Jamjodhpur Live Market Portal

A modern, responsive agricultural marketplace website designed for APMC Jamjodhpur (Marketing Yard Jamjodhpur). The platform connects farmers, traders, and buyers through real-time market information, crop arrivals, auction services, and digital trading facilities.

📌 Project Overview

APMC Jamjodhpur Live Market Portal is a digital platform built to modernize agricultural trading and improve transparency in market operations. The website provides farmers and traders with access to live commodity prices, crop arrivals, auction updates, trader registration, and e-NAM integration services.

The platform aims to simplify agricultural commerce by delivering accurate market information and a user-friendly experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
